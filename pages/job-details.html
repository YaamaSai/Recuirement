About the Role

We are looking for a talented professional to join our core product team. You will be responsible for architecting and building scalable, performant solutions using modern technologies. You'll collaborate closely with our designers, product managers, and engineering teams to deliver a world-class experience to millions of users globally.

If you are passionate about performance, accessibility, and clean code, we want to talk to you!

Key Responsibilities

  • Architect and develop complex components and application features.
  • Collaborate with UI/UX designers to translate mockups into highly responsive, interactive experiences.
  • Optimize applications for maximum speed, scalability, and accessibility.
  • Participate in code reviews, write comprehensive unit and integration tests.
  • Mentor junior team members and contribute to our internal standards.

Requirements & Qualifications

  • 3+ years of professional experience in your respective field.
  • Deep expertise in modern development frameworks and languages.
  • Strong proficiency with problem solving and system design.
  • Excellent communication skills and the ability to work in a fully remote, asynchronous environment.
  • Solid understanding of performance optimization techniques.

Benefits & Perks

  • Competitive base salary plus equity options.
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
  • Unlimited PTO policy and generous parental leave.
  • $2,000 annual home office setup stipend.
  • Annual learning and development budget.
  • Flexible working hours and 100% remote flexibility.
