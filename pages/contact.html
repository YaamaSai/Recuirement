FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the hiring process usually take?

On average, our clients fill roles within 2–4 weeks from initial posting to offer acceptance. Our AI matching significantly accelerates the screening stage.

Is there a cost for candidates to use the platform?

No. RecruitPro is completely free for job seekers. Our revenue comes from employer subscriptions and placement services.

Can I integrate RecruitPro with my existing ATS?

Yes! Our Professional and Enterprise plans include integrations with popular ATS systems like Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, and more.

Do you offer temporary or contract staffing?

Absolutely. We support permanent, contract, and temporary placements across all industries and seniority levels.
